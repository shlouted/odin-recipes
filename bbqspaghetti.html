BBQ Spaghetti Recipe

Description

BBQ SPaghetti is truly one of the most delicous dishes I have ever tasted! It is a mix on the tradtitional Italian food spaghetti and the sweet tangy taste of southern BBQ.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 diced green pepper
  • 1 diced yellow onion
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 14 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 14 oz can petite diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup your fav BBQ sauce I use Gate's original
  • 1 lb cooked spaghetti
  • 1 lb pulled pork
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cumin

Steps

  1. Heat Olive oil in large saucepan over medium heat. Add the onion and bell pepper, cook stirring occasionally for 3-4 minutes or until soft. Add minced garlic and cook stirring constantly for 1-2 more minutes.
  2. Add the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, BBQ Sauce, pulled pork, and seasonings to the pan.
  3. Stir to combine, and simmer for 20-30 minutes over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  4. Prepare pasta according to package directions and drain. Stir in 2 Tbsp melted butter.
  5. Pour sauce over pasta, and toss gently to combine. Drizzle additional BBQ sauce if desired.
